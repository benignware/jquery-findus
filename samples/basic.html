Basic Usage

Initialize jquery-findus

$(function() {
  $('.findus').findus();
})

Content

The content of the infowindow can be customized by providing html as dom children or as an escaped string using the data-content-attribute.
If location or address was not specified, the location will be geocoded from the content. Phone numbers, emails and urls are stripped from the geocoded string. A geocodeable string can be explicitly defined by wrapping it into an address-tag 

<div class="findus">
  <h5>Find us here</h5>
  <p>
    8411 Market Street<br/>
    San Francisco<br/>
    CA 94103
  </p>
  <p>
    Email <b><a href="mailto:mail@example.de">mail@example.de</a></b><br/>
    Phone <b>+44 (123) 4567890</b><br/>
    Homepage <b><a href="http://example.com">http://example.com</a></b><br/>
  </p>
</div>
Find us here

8411 Market Street
San Francisco
CA 94103

Email mail@example.de
Phone +44 (123) 4567890
Homepage http://example.com

Avoid geocoding

To avoid geocoding service completely, you should provide both, a location as well as some content to show up into the info-window. 

<div class="findus" data-latitude="40.7142700" data-longitude="-74.0059700">
  <h5>Find us here</h5>
  8411 Market Street<br/>
  San Francisco<br/>
  CA 94103
</div>
Find us here
8411 Market Street
San Francisco
CA 94103

Data Attributes

Use data-attributes to setup the component. Target object-options by using prefixes, such as 'marker-icon'.

<div class="findus"> data-auto-show="false"> data-marker-icon="information.png">
  <h5>Find us here</h5>
  8411 Market Street<br/>
  San Francisco
  CA 94103
</div>
Find us here
8411 Market Street
San Francisco
CA 94103
